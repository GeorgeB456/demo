Some projects I've been working on lately:

| Design research blog
'Modern Heraldry' is a digital blog interpreting data and analytics to analyse the impact of information and technology on contemporary culture. It centres around the concept of visual identity as it relates to Medieval heraldic practices.

| A website for metal music fans
'Breakdown' was an experiment in web design using HTML and CSS in the 1st semester last year. It exists in the cathartic overlap between metal music and the increasing number of 'rage rooms' opening around New Zealand, and is an online attempt to replicate the feeeling of catharsis.

| ChromaLogic
'ChromaLogic' is an experimentation in interactive design, exploring the concept of colour-time synesthesia. The conceptual website asks the user a series of abstract questions that play on the userâ€™s gut response and subconscious associations with colour and time. The question is, essentially, "What colour is time?".
